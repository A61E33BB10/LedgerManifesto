\section{Reconciliation Failure Mode Taxonomy}
\label{sec:appC}\label{app:reconciliation}
%==============================================================================

Each class of internal reconciliation break is classified against the architecture as \emph{structurally unreachable}, \emph{easier to detect}, or \emph{addressed by design}, for activity recorded in the ledger. Three modes are structurally unreachable: the architecture makes the divergent state unrepresentable. Six are easier to detect but not eliminable, because they depend on an external actor the ledger only mirrors. Securities-lending breaks are eliminated by design. Operational failures outside the architecture---incomplete booking, duplicate booking through external channels, stale-data ingestion---remain possible and require operational controls.
