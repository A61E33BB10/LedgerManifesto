\section{European SBL Worked Example: GMSLA~2010 Title Transfer with Rehypothecation}
\label{sec:appH}
\label{app:eu-sbl-example}

A complete European securities loan under GMSLA~2010 (title transfer): non-cash collateral, rehypothecation, daily mark-to-market, a manufactured dividend, a partial recall, and full return. Every move is a single-coordinate write of the six-coordinate position vector (Section~\ref{sec:gpm}, Principle~\ref{princ:single-coord}); conservation is verified at each step. This is the title-transfer correctness witness; the pledge counterpart is Appendix~\ref{app:us-sbl-example}.

\subsection{Participants and Setup}

\begin{center}\small
\begin{tabular}{l l p{7.2cm}}
\toprule
\textbf{Entity} & \textbf{Role} & \textbf{Description} \\
\midrule
CALPERS\_EU & Lender (beneficial owner) & Holds 500{,}000 TotalEnergies (ISIN FR0000120271). Agent lender BNY~Mellon. \\
GOLDMAN & Borrower & Prime broker; borrows TTE to cover a client short. \\
HEDGE\_FUND & Goldman's client & Short seller; sells the borrowed TTE to market. \\
MARKET & Market buyer & Virtual wallet for the open market. \\
COUNTERPARTY\_C & Third party & Receives rehypothecated Bund collateral in a separate repo. \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\end{center}

\noindent\textbf{Loan terms.} 500{,}000 TotalEnergies (TTE), open loan, fee 25~bps p.a.\ (ACT/360), non-cash collateral (German Bund, ISIN DE0001102580), margin 102\%, GMSLA~2010 title transfer. Under title transfer Goldman takes ownership of the Bund collateral and may rehypothecate it; SFTR Article~15 documentation is required.

\noindent\textbf{Units.} TTE (equity), BUND (German Bund), EUR (cash). Position vector $(\mathrm{own},\,\allowbreak\mathrm{onloan},\,\allowbreak\mathrm{borr},\,\allowbreak\mathrm{coll\_post},\,\allowbreak\mathrm{coll\_recv},\,\allowbreak\mathrm{coll\_rehyp})$, abbreviated own, onl, borr, c\_p, c\_r, c\_rh.

\begin{remark}
Loan, collateral, and exposure values follow the valuation rules of the valuation satellite specification (IBP-163, IBP-129, IBP-191); the figures are stated here as inputs, not re-derived. Intermediate quantities are computed in exact integer minor units; round-half-to-even (IEEE~754) applies only at the settlement-instruction projection boundary.
\end{remark}

\subsection{Step 1: Loan Initiation (Day 0)}
\label{sec:eu-step1}
%----------------------------------------------------------------------

TTE \EUR{58.50}; Bund \EUR{98.50} per \EUR{100} nominal.
\[
\mathrm{LV} = 500{,}000 \times 58.50 = \text{\EUR{29,250,000}}, \qquad
\mathrm{RCV} = \mathrm{LV} \times 102\% = \text{\EUR{29,835,000}}.
\]
\[
Q_{\mathrm{Bund}} = \left\lceil \tfrac{29{,}835{,}000}{98.50} \right\rceil = 302{,}894 \;\text{(units of \EUR{100} nominal)},
\]
market value $302{,}894 \times 98.50 = \text{\EUR{29,835,059}}$ (slight over-collateralisation from rounding up).

\begin{lstlisting}
tau_1 = Transaction(type = SETTLEMENT, day = 0):
  -- Securities leg (TTE)
  Move 1: CALPERS_EU  onloan    += 500,000   [onloan]
  Move 2: GOLDMAN     borr      += 500,000   [borr]
  -- Collateral leg (BUND) -- title transfer
  Move 3: GOLDMAN     own       -= 302,894   [own]
  Move 4: CALPERS_EU  coll_recv += 302,894   [coll_recv]
\end{lstlisting}

\noindent Move~3 writes $\mathrm{own}$ (not $\mathrm{coll\_post}$): under title transfer the poster transfers \emph{ownership}, so Goldman's Bund ownership decreases and CalPERS holds the Bunds as received collateral. This is the defining distinction from the pledge regime (Appendix~\ref{app:us-sbl-example}). Virtual wallets absorb the contra-entries for both legs.

\begin{center}\small
\begin{tabular}{l l r r r r r r l}
\toprule
\textbf{Entity} & \textbf{Unit} & own & onl & borr & c\_p & c\_r & c\_rh & avail \\
\midrule
CALPERS\_EU & TTE & 500{,}000 & 500{,}000 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\
CALPERS\_EU & BUND & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 302{,}894 & 0 & 0 \\
GOLDMAN & TTE & 0 & 0 & 500{,}000 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 500{,}000 \\
GOLDMAN & BUND & $-302{,}894$ & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & $-302{,}894$ \\
Virtual$_{C{\leftrightarrow}G}$ & TTE & \multicolumn{7}{l}{$-500{,}000$ (securities delivery contra)} \\
Virtual$_{\mathrm{mkt}}$ & BUND & \multicolumn{7}{l}{$+302{,}894$ (Bund delivery contra)} \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\end{center}

\noindent\textbf{Conservation (TTE).} $500{,}000_C + 500{,}000_{G,\mathrm{borr}} - 500{,}000_{\mathrm{Virt}} - 500{,}000_{\mathrm{Cust}} = 0$. \checkmark\quad
\textbf{P18.} CalPERS $\mathrm{own}(\text{TTE}) = 500{,}000$, unchanged. \checkmark\quad
\textbf{SFTR.} NEWT.

\subsection{Step 2: Delivery to Hedge Fund and Short Sale (Day 0)}
\label{sec:eu-step2}
%----------------------------------------------------------------------

\textbf{Locate (EU SSR Article 12(1)).} Before the short sale HEDGE\_FUND satisfies the locate obligation; the borrow from CalPERS meets Article~12(1)(a) (the person ``has borrowed the share''). Locate is a pre-trade validation the executor checks before $\tau_3$; it generates no moves and changes no coordinate.

\begin{lstlisting}
tau_2 = Transaction(type = SETTLEMENT, day = 0):  -- Goldman -> Hedge Fund
  Move 1: GOLDMAN    own -= 500,000    [own]
  Move 2: HEDGE_FUND own += 500,000    [own]

tau_3 = Transaction(type = SETTLEMENT, day = 0):  -- Hedge Fund short sale to market
  Move 1: HEDGE_FUND own -= 500,000        [own]
  Move 2: MARKET     own += 500,000        [own]
  Move 3: MARKET     own -= 29,250,000     [own, EUR]
  Move 4: HEDGE_FUND own += 29,250,000     [own, EUR]
\end{lstlisting}

\noindent Goldman's $\mathrm{borr}$ is untouched: it tracks the return obligation, not possession.

\begin{center}\small
\begin{tabular}{l l r r r r r r l}
\toprule
\textbf{Entity} & \textbf{Unit} & own & onl & borr & c\_p & c\_r & c\_rh & avail \\
\midrule
CALPERS\_EU & TTE & 500{,}000 & 500{,}000 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\
GOLDMAN & TTE & $-500{,}000$ & 0 & 500{,}000 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\
HEDGE\_FUND & TTE & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\
MARKET & TTE & 500{,}000 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 500{,}000 \\
HEDGE\_FUND & EUR & 29{,}250{,}000 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 29{,}250{,}000 \\

\noindent Goldman is flat in available shares ($-500{,}000 + 500{,}000_{\mathrm{borr}} = 0$) but short in ownership: the economic short passed through from the hedge fund.

\noindent\textbf{Conservation (TTE).} $500{,}000_C - 500{,}000_G + 500{,}000_M - 500{,}000_{\mathrm{Virt}} = 0$. \checkmark

\subsection{Step 3: Rehypothecation of Bund Collateral (Day 1)}
\label{sec:eu-step3}
%----------------------------------------------------------------------

Goldman, owning the Bunds under title transfer, re-uses 302{,}894 of them as collateral in a separate repo with COUNTERPARTY\_C. P19 (rehypothecation regime compliance) requires SFTR Article~15 re-use documentation: an operational compliance flag, not a move.

\begin{lstlisting}
tau_4a = Transaction(type = RECLASSIFICATION, day = 1):  -- within CalPERS vector
  Move 1: CALPERS_EU  coll_recv  -= 302,894   [coll_recv, BUND]
  Move 2: CALPERS_EU  coll_rehyp += 302,894   [coll_rehyp, BUND]

tau_4b = Transaction(type = SETTLEMENT, day = 1):  -- onward delivery to COUNTERPARTY_C
  Move 1: GOLDMAN        own -= 302,894        [own, BUND]
  Move 2: COUNTERPARTY_C own += 302,894        [own, BUND]
  Move 3: COUNTERPARTY_C own -= 29,835,059     [own, EUR]
  Move 4: GOLDMAN        own += 29,835,059     [own, EUR]
\end{lstlisting}

\noindent Goldman's Bund $\mathrm{own}$ moves $-302{,}894 \to -605{,}788$: doubly short, having delivered Bunds to CalPERS and to COUNTERPARTY\_C, and owing both. The reclassification records the re-use inside CalPERS's vector without changing CalPERS's total Bund holding.

\begin{tabular}{l l r r r r r r l}

\midrule
CALPERS\_EU & BUND & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 302{,}894 & 0 \\
GOLDMAN & BUND & $-605{,}788$ & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & $-605{,}788$ \\
COUNTERPARTY\_C & BUND & 302{,}894 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 302{,}894 \\
GOLDMAN & EUR & 29{,}835{,}059 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 29{,}835{,}059 \\
COUNTERPARTY\_C & EUR & $-29{,}835{,}059$ & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & $-29{,}835{,}059$ \\
\bottomrule

\noindent\textbf{Conservation (BUND).} $302{,}894_C - 605{,}788_G + 302{,}894_{CC} + 302{,}894_{\mathrm{Virt}} - 302{,}894_{\mathrm{Cust}} = 0$. \checkmark\quad
\textbf{P19.} Regime \texttt{TITLE\_TRANSFER} permits rehypothecation; Art.~15 flag set. \checkmark

\subsection{Step 4: Mark-to-Market (Day 3)}
\label{sec:eu-step4}
%----------------------------------------------------------------------

TTE \EUR{60.25}; Bund \EUR{98.20}.
\[
\mathrm{LV}_3 = 500{,}000 \times 60.25 \times 102\% = \text{\EUR{30,727,500}}, \qquad
\text{collateral} = 302{,}894 \times 98.20 = \text{\EUR{29,744,189}}.
\]
\[
\text{Exposure} = 30{,}727{,}500 - 29{,}744{,}189 = \text{\EUR{983,311}}, \qquad
Q_{\mathrm{add}} = \left\lceil \tfrac{983{,}311}{98.20} \right\rceil = 10{,}014 \;\text{units}.
\]
The lender is under-collateralised; a margin call delivers additional Bunds.

\begin{lstlisting}
tau_5 = Transaction(type = MARGIN_CALL, day = 3):
  Move 1: GOLDMAN     own       -= 10,014   [own, BUND]
  Move 2: CALPERS_EU  coll_recv += 10,014   [coll_recv, BUND]
\end{lstlisting}

\noindent The addition enters $\mathrm{coll\_recv}$ (not $\mathrm{coll\_rehyp}$): it is not yet re-used.

\begin{center}\small
\begin{tabular}{l l r r r r r r}
\toprule
\textbf{Entity} & \textbf{Unit} & own & onl & borr & c\_p & c\_r & c\_rh \\
\midrule
CALPERS\_EU & BUND & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 10{,}014 & 302{,}894 \\
GOLDMAN & BUND & $-615{,}802$ & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\
COUNTERPARTY\_C & BUND & 302{,}894 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabular}
\end{center}

\noindent\textbf{P13.} CalPERS holds $312{,}908$ Bunds $\times\,98.20 = \text{\EUR{30,727,566}} \geq \text{\EUR{30,727,500}}$ required. \checkmark\quad
\textbf{Fee accrual (Days 0--3).} Day~0 base $29{,}250{,}000 \times 0.0025/360 = \text{\EUR{203.13}}$/day; from Day~3, $30{,}125{,}000 \times 0.0025/360 = \text{\EUR{209.20}}$/day. Running total \EUR{818.58}.\quad
\textbf{SFTR.} VALU.

\subsection{Step 5: Manufactured Dividend (Day 7)}
\label{sec:eu-step5}
%----------------------------------------------------------------------

TotalEnergies pays \EUR{1.25} per share. CalPERS retains economic ownership; Goldman pays a manufactured dividend of $500{,}000 \times 1.25 = \text{\EUR{625,000}}$. The manufactured payment is a contractual GMSLA payment, not a distribution, and its net amount may differ from the gross dividend under the applicable withholding-tax treaty.

\begin{lstlisting}
tau_6 = Transaction(type = CORP_ACTION, day = 7):
  Move 1: GOLDMAN     own -= 625,000    [own, EUR]
  Move 2: CALPERS_EU  own += 625,000    [own, EUR]
\end{lstlisting}

CALPERS\_EU & 625{,}000 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\
GOLDMAN & 29{,}210{,}059 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \\

\noindent\textbf{Conservation (EUR).} $625{,}000_C + 29{,}210{,}059_G + 29{,}250{,}000_{HF} - 29{,}835{,}059_{CC} + \text{virtual offsets} = 0$. \checkmark

\subsection{Step 6: Partial Recall and Return (Days 10--12)}
\label{sec:eu-step6}
%----------------------------------------------------------------------

CalPERS sells 200{,}000 TTE; BNY~Mellon recalls 200{,}000 shares. Recall notice (IBP-328) reaches Goldman on Day~10; settlement at $T+2$ (Euronext Paris) on Day~12.

\begin{lstlisting}
tau_7 = Transaction(type = RECALL, day = 10):
  -- Move-less: empty move list (vacuously conserved); carries only a state delta.
  -- SBL state ACTIVE -> RECALLED; recall_qty = 200,000; recall_date = Day 10
\end{lstlisting}

\noindent\textbf{Day 12.} TTE \EUR{59.80}; Bund \EUR{98.30}. Remaining loan $300{,}000$:
\[
\mathrm{LV}_{12} = 300{,}000 \times 59.80 \times 102\% = \text{\EUR{18,298,800}}.
\]
Collateral held $312{,}908 \times 98.30 = \text{\EUR{30,758,856}}$; excess \EUR{12,460,056}:
\[
Q_{\mathrm{release}} = \left\lfloor \tfrac{12{,}460{,}056}{98.30} \right\rfloor = 126{,}755 \;\text{Bund units}.
\]
Remaining $312{,}908 - 126{,}755 = 186{,}153$ Bunds, value $186{,}153 \times 98.30 = \text{\EUR{18,298,835}} \geq \mathrm{LV}_{12}$ (P13 \checkmark). Release draws from $\mathrm{coll\_recv}$ first (10{,}014), then $\mathrm{coll\_rehyp}$ (116{,}741).

\begin{lstlisting}
tau_8 = Transaction(type = PARTIAL_RETURN, day = 12):
  -- Securities return (TTE)
  Move 1: GOLDMAN     borr       -= 200,000   [borr]
  Move 2: CALPERS_EU  onloan     -= 200,000   [onloan]
  -- Collateral release (BUND): coll_recv then coll_rehyp
  Move 3: CALPERS_EU  coll_recv  -= 10,014    [coll_recv]
  Move 4: GOLDMAN     own        += 10,014    [own]
  Move 5: CALPERS_EU  coll_rehyp -= 116,741   [coll_rehyp]
  Move 6: GOLDMAN     own        += 116,741   [own]

tau_8b = Transaction(type = SETTLEMENT, day = 12):  -- Goldman unwinds repo leg
  Move 1: COUNTERPARTY_C own -= 116,741        [own, BUND]
  Move 2: GOLDMAN        own += 116,741        [own, BUND]
  Move 3: GOLDMAN        own -= 11,473,439     [own, EUR]
  Move 4: COUNTERPARTY_C own += 11,473,439     [own, EUR]
\end{lstlisting}

\noindent Releasing 116{,}741 from $\mathrm{coll\_rehyp}$ forces Goldman to recall those Bunds from COUNTERPARTY\_C first ($\tau_{8b}$).

\subsection{Step 7: Full Return and Settlement (Day 20)}
\label{sec:eu-step7}
%----------------------------------------------------------------------

Remaining 300{,}000 TTE returned. TTE \EUR{61.00}; Bund \EUR{98.40}.

\begin{lstlisting}
tau_9 = Transaction(type = FULL_RETURN, day = 20):
  Move 1: GOLDMAN     borr       -= 300,000   [borr]
  Move 2: CALPERS_EU  onloan     -= 300,000   [onloan]
  Move 3: CALPERS_EU  coll_rehyp -= 186,153   [coll_rehyp]

tau_9b = Transaction(type = SETTLEMENT, day = 20):  -- unwind remaining repo
  Move 1: COUNTERPARTY_C own -= 186,153        [own, BUND]
  Move 2: GOLDMAN        own += 186,153        [own, BUND]
  Move 3: GOLDMAN        own -= 18,308,845     [own, EUR]
  Move 4: COUNTERPARTY_C own += 18,308,845     [own, EUR]
\end{lstlisting}

\noindent\textbf{PnL.}
\begin{itemize}[nosep]
\item \textbf{CalPERS}: \EUR{3,548.40} fee $+$ \EUR{625,000} manufactured dividend; TTE position unchanged, unrealised $500{,}000 \times (61.00 - 58.50) = \text{\EUR{1,250,000}}$.

\item \textbf{Hedge Fund}: short PnL $(58.50 - 61.00) \times 500{,}000 = -\text{\EUR{1,250,000}}$; must buy back at \EUR{61.00} to close.
\end{itemize}
