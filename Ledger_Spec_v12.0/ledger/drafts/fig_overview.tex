\caption{The single source of truth. Contracts emit moves that are appended to one
immutable move stream; every internal view---balances, PnL, the balance sheet, and
reports---is a one-way \emph{projection} of that stream. The arrows are derivations, not
synchronisations: there is no second internal record, hence nothing to reconcile within the
boundary. External authorities lie outside the boundary and are reconciled against only at
its edge.}
